Our History A Legacy Built on Service and Reliability
1990

The Beginning

Sea-Jet was founded in 1990 with a clear purpose: to make shipping simple, reliable and seamless. From day one, we focused on skilled people, strong global partnerships and dependable transport capabilities. Over the years, we have moved thousands of containers and air shipments worldwide, each handled with precision and care.

Expanding to Serve More Customers

As demand for our services grew, we expanded our operations with full-service branches in Cebu and Pampanga. This expansion strengthened our reach across Southern Visayas and Northern Luzon, allowing us to support customers with faster, more efficient logistics solutions

1992
2017

Strengthening Our Global Network

Sea-Jet continued building strong partnerships across major ports and key cities worldwide. With access to advanced logistics information systems and our own fleet of vehicles for trucking and cartage, we enhanced our ability to provide full visibility, seamless coordination and on-time deliveries

2025

Resilient, Adaptive and Moving Forward

The logistics landscape changed significantly during the pandemic, but Sea-Jet remained steadfast in its commitment to clients. Despite global disruptions, port closures and capacity challenges, our team adapted quickly by leveraging our network, optimizing routes and maintaining clear communication to keep cargo moving.

Today, Sea-Jet continues to serve customers with the same resilience, responsiveness an dedication that carried us through the most challenging years. We remain focused on delivering stability, confidence and consistent service in an ever-changing world.
