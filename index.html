Our Membership
Professional Forwarders International (PFI)

PFI is an organization representing professional freight forwarders in the Philippines. PFI works with government and industry partners to enhance forwarding services and support efficient cargo movement.

International Freight Forwarders Network

JC TRANS is an international logistics network connecting freight forwarders worldwide. JC TRANS works with member forwarders and industry partners to support global shipments and strengthen end-to-end logistics services.

Uconnect World Wide

UConnect Worldwide is a global network connecting independent freight forwarders and logistics companies. UConnect works with its members and industry partners to support reliable, coordinated and efficient international cargo services.

Our Affiliations
Department of Trade and Industry Philippines

DTI is the government agency responsible for promoting and regulating trade and industry in the Philippines. It works with businesses and stakeholders to support industry growth and consumer protection.

Confederation of Truckers Association of the Philippines (CTAP)

CTAP is a major alliance of trucking organizations in the Philippines. CTAP works with government agencies, port authorities and private groups to ensure smooth trucking operations.

The Philippine Economic Zone Authority

PEZA is the government agency that promotes investments and oversees special economic zones, ensuring efficient operations and supporting economic growth.

Civil Aeronautics Board (CAB)

CAB is the government agency that regulates the economic aspects of air transportation in the Philippines. CAB works with airlines, airports and government partners to ensure safe, fair and efficient air transport services.

Philippine Multimodal Transport and Logistics Association

PMTLA is an organization representing multimodal transport and logistics companies in the Philippines. PMTLA works with government and industry partners to improve logistics efficiency and support seamless cargo movement.

Philippine Exporters Confederation, Inc.

PHILEXPORT is a national organization representing Philippine exporters. PHILEXPORT works with government agencies, industries and trade partners to promote exports and support the growth of the export sector
